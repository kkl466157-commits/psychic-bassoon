How this works

1. What is Supply Chain Management? ⬇️
Supply Chain Management (SCM) is the end-to-end coordination of the movement of goods — from raw materials to the final product reaching the customer. Effective SCM reduces costs, improves speed, and ensures customer satisfaction.
2. Key Processes in SCM ⬇️
SCM includes procurement, production planning, inventory management, transportation, and last-mile delivery. By managing these processes efficiently, businesses can achieve smooth operations and reduced wastage.
3. Technology & Tracking ⬇️
At GREEN TRACK, we use innovative technology and real-time tracking systems to monitor goods across the entire supply chain. This ensures transparency, efficiency, and faster decision-making.
4. Why Supply Chain Management Matters? ⬇️
Efficient supply chain management helps businesses remain competitive by lowering costs, speeding up deliveries, and improving service quality. It creates a seamless flow of goods that keeps businesses running smoothly.
