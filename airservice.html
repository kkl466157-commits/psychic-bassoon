How this works

1. What is Air Freight? ⬇️
Air freight is the fastest method of transporting goods by airplane. It is ideal for urgent, high-value, or perishable shipments.
2. Which goods are suitable for Air Freight? ⬇️
Air freight is best suited for high-value items, perishable goods, urgent deliveries, and fragile shipments.
